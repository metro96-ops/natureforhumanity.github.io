Who We Are

Nature for Humanity Initiative Ltd. is a registered community-based organization based in Uganda, working at the intersection of environment, inclusion, and sustainability. We are driven by a belief that the health of our planet and the well-being of humanity are inseparable.

Our Mission

To empower communities through nature-based solutions that promote renewable energy, water conservation, gender equity, youth engagement, and climate resilience.
