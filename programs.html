Our Programs

We connect people, purpose, and planet through practical, measurable climate action and community empowerment initiatives.

Clean Energy Access

We deploy affordable solar energy systems to schools, health centers, and households, reducing carbon emissions while improving education, safety, and livelihoods.

  • Community solar hubs
  • Solar for schools,maternity centers & markets
  • Installing Charging Stations and introducing EV buses ,Bikes to uplift local economy
  • Rural electrification for resilience

Reforestation & Carbon Offsetting

Our reforestation program restores degraded ecosystems and strengthens community-based carbon markets. Indigenous trees and agroforestry create long-term income for local farmers while absorbing CO₂.

  • Community tree nurseries
  • Carbon credit training and Enviromental awareness
  • Soil and water conservation

Biodiversity & Pollinator Protection

Bees and pollinators are critical to food systems and biodiversity. We support wild habitats and create pollinator-friendly landscapes.

  • Bee habitat restoration
  • Community apiary development
  • Climate-smart beekeeping
  • Pesticide usuage education to local farmers

Women in Sustainability

Women are the backbone of climate resilience. Our programs train women and marginalized youths in renewable energy, water management, and green entrepreneurship.

  • Training in solar and agriculture
  • Micro-enterprise development
  • Leadership and inclusion networks
  • Tailoring and football coach courses offered to the youth

Sports for Climate & Peace

Football is a platform for climate education, teamwork, and peacebuilding. Through youth tournaments and environmental campaigns, sports become a unifying force for change.

  • Football for environmental awareness
  • Youth leadership through sports
  • Health and peace education
  • Sports for all gender

Environmental Education & Digital Literacy

We empower children and marginalized youth with digital and environmental education. From coding clubs to tree-planting drives, we equip the next generation to protect and innovate for a sustainable future.

  • Green schools initiative
  • Digital climate learning
  • Youth mentorship
  • Forest computer labs for Computer Education

Water Access & WASH

Safe water access is fundamental to dignity, health, and climate resilience. We support borehole rehabilitation, community water points, and WASH training for households and schools.

  • Community water infrastructure
  • Hygiene training and gender-sensitive WASH
  • Climate risk management for water systems
