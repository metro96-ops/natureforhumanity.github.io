Our Programs

Community-powered solutions that restore ecosystems, strengthen resilience, and deliver measurable climate impact.

Community Reforestation & Carbon Offsets

We restore native forests and agroforestry systems that capture CO₂, cool local microclimates, protect soils, and support livelihoods. Every planting season feeds our living carbon network.

Goal: 100,000 native trees and verified community carbon gains by 2028.

Join This Work

Water & Sanitation for Life

We rehabilitate boreholes, harvest rainwater, and train communities on safe hygiene. Efficient water systems reduce the energy and carbon costs of access and treatment.

Goal: Safe, sustainable water for 25,000 people.

Join This Work

Renewable Energy for All

Solar for schools and clinics, cleaner cooking, and practical training for local technicians. Replacing fossil and wood fuels cuts emissions and unlocks opportunity.

Goal: Reliable clean energy for 10,000+ households by 2026.

Join This Work

Biodiversity & Wildlife Protection

Protecting pollinators, birds, and habitats through native planting, corridor restoration, and community stewardship. Healthy ecosystems are the foundation of climate resilience.

Goal: Thriving pollinator corridors and bird-friendly landscapes.

Join This Work

Women, Children & Community Leadership

We support climate-smart livelihoods, micro-enterprise, and local governance. Programs welcome safe participation from community members of different backgrounds and experiences.

Goal: 500+ local leaders equipped to drive climate solutions.

Join This Work

Youth for a Green Future

Bootcamps, exchanges, and service projects that channel creativity into climate solutions. Youth volunteers help track trees and energy projects with simple carbon mapping tools.

Goal: 1,000 youth engaged in hands-on sustainability.

Join This Work

Sports for Sustainability & Unity

Through football and community sports, we build teamwork, mental wellness, and eco-action. “Play for the Planet” matches link goals scored to trees planted and event offsets.

Goal: 5,000 youth engaged through eco-sports by 2027.

Join This Work

Climate Education & Research

Eco-clubs, community learning, and place-based research that blends lived experience with practical science. Carbon literacy is woven through all curricula.

Goal: Climate literacy for 50,000 learners by 2028.
